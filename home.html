About This Project

Akan names come from Ghanaian culture, where children are named according to the day of the week they were born. This application calculates your birthday and assigns you the correct Akan name based on your gender.

How It Works

  1. Enter your birth date
  2. Select your gender
  3. Click generate
  4. Get your Akan name instantly
